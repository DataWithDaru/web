Tableau

According to Gartner Tableau and PowerBI are the two main leaders in the business intelligence platform market. Tableau was introduced in 2003 and grew into one of the most popular BI tools. The question would be, why Tableau over PowerBI? The reality of the situation is they do the majority of things the same, they have beautiful visualizations, they integrate with various data sources, they are user-friendly, easy to integrate with other applications. My approach is simple: Not all clients need the same thing, therefore, I need to be flexible in what I am capable of doing, however, I would still call myself a PowerBI enthusiast. Nevertheless, take a look!

Power BI

PowerBI has been the cool kid on the block since Microsoft launched it in 2011 and added it to Office 365 in 2013. By the time I was introduced to the industry of business intelligence in 2019, I noticed how it just stood out from the rest of its competitors but the majority of our clients at that time did not adopt it, yet. PowerBI, in my opinion, is the leading BI solution for the majority of the companies out there, once you see the potential, it's hard to go back, especially if most people are already onboard themselves. It integrates seamlessly, it has great AI capabilities, it's secure, it's beautiful, and is definitely worth looking into if you have not already done so.
